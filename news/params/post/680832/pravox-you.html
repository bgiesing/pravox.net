Pravox YOU: Our new platform!

January 7, 2016 at 4:06 am, No comments
As we roll along the first month of Pravox Networks, we are going to be improving multiple aspects from design, our homepage, and of course our actual stations!

The latest change is a new platform we are calling Pravox YOU!

What is it?

Simply put, we now run our own servers using Amazon EC2 and CyTube (a platform for creating what similar channels to ours). Unlike our old provider, CyTube allows you to run your own site dedicated to your channels. 

What's New/Different?

ACCOUNTS

  • Accounts are no longer required to add videos!
  • Stations.tv account doesn't work, if you want to join, resign up.

MOAR CONTENT

  • Vimeo
  • SoundCloud
  • Dailymotion
  • Google Drive (not recommended as can't be viewed on mobile!)
  • Google+ (Post the video on Google+, then right click the preview and copy the link.)
  • Any Direct Link (MP3/MP4/FLV/WebM/OGG; great for Podcasts)

EASIER CONTROL

  • Playlist Shuffle
  • Temporary/Permanent Videos so you can always play something without playing a one-time video 50 times.
  • Layouts with Chat Only/Video Only
  • No duplicate videos allowed!
  • Max Length for Channel Owners


Join now by clicking the channel logos on our homepage or click below to create your own channel!

Pravox YOU

FAQ

Why is it YOU then?

Well, because anybody can make their own that will instantly be a partner station! Also, it's still controlled by you.

If you own it, then you can censor me right?

Technically yes, but I won't. I believe in a free and open internet and it should all be allowed. Our main channels still don't allow explicit content but you can make one if you want!
